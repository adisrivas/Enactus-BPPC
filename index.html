WHO WE ARE
Enactus is the world’s largest experiential learning platform dedicated to creating a better world while developing the next generation of entrepreneurial leaders and social innovators. The Enactus network of global business, academic and student leaders are unified by our vision—to create a better, more sustainable world.

Enactus, formely known as Students In Free Enterprise (SIFE) is an international non-profit organisation of students present in over 1600 universities spread across 39 Countries and has over 67,000 students as active members.

Enactus aims at making use of Entrepreneurial Action for others to create a better world for all of Us. It is integrating business skills and a desire for a social cause along with the nation’s youth coming together on a single platform to be a part of the change. Enactus students take up community outreach projects to transform lives of people in need and shape a better world, using the power of entrepreneurial action.

With a head for business and a heart for the world, we live our values of integrity, passion, innovation and collaboration.

OUR STORY

WHAT WE DO

Birla Institute of Technology and Science, Pilani is a premier institute for higher education in India. Founded in 1964, since then BITS, Pilani has played a leading role in scientific research and development in India. Enactus BITS, Pilani was started in the year of 2016. Since then, we have consistently taken up several social entrepreneurship projects.
