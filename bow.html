BEST OUT OF THE WASTE

Enactus BITS Pilani students chapter took up a project in October 2018.

It was started in order to create a self-sustainable business for the people of the nearby village, Jherli around the campus. The project was initiated with an aim to expand the business opportunities and provide employment to many more villages.

To achieve the purpose of establishing an SHG, a Best Out of Waste (BOW) project was initiated. It is to help the people developing a steady source of their income. So regular visits were made initially to the villages to check upon the completion and proper functioning of the project work. The raw materials used involved waste cans, bottles and newspapers which are either thrown or discarded by the people. A collection drive was, for first, set up on our campus to collect the raw material. The collected raw material is provided to the people and a workplace has been made available by the village head. The villagers are very enthusiastic. With their dedication to work, the people are being taught the various ways to put these raw materials into use. The products prepared are decorative items like diyas, pen stand and tie-dye t-shirts.
